Troubleshooting

  • Camera / barcode: Settings → Privacy & Security → Camera.
  • Exports / storage: free space on device; for manual backup use Share to save to Files or iCloud. Auto-export writes under the app Documents path in Settings — if it fails, retry from the alert or export manually.
  • Missing data after reinstall: restore from your latest backup bundle or CSV path documented in-app.
  • Crash on launch: update iOS, restart the device, then reinstall if needed.
